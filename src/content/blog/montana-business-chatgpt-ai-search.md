## What Is AI Search?

AI search is when someone opens ChatGPT, Claude, or Perplexity and asks a question instead of typing into Google. Rather than scrolling through a list of links, they get one direct answer. A business is either mentioned in that answer or it isn't.

If your business isn't set up for AI search, it won't be mentioned.

## Why Montana Businesses Need to Pay Attention

Younger customers aren't searching the way they used to. One in three Gen Z consumers and one in four Millennials now go to AI tools first when deciding where to spend money. These are the people planning Montana fishing trips, looking for a guide service, or figuring out which Bozeman brewery to hit on a Friday night.

And this isn't just a younger generation thing. Fourteen percent of all consumers across every age group now prefer ChatGPT over Google for search. Here's what makes that number matter: 93% of those AI search sessions end without the user clicking a single link. The business that gets named in the answer wins the customer. Everyone else gets skipped.

## Who Is Already Searching This Way?

Travelers planning outdoor trips are the heaviest AI search users of any group. Sixty-two percent of Millennials and Gen Z use AI to plan travel compared to 35% of older travelers. Among people booking lodging specifically, 78% say it is very important that a property shows up in AI recommendations. If a hotel, rental, or guide service isn't visible to AI tools, it's being left out of that conversation entirely.

It goes beyond travel too. Locals in the 14 to 29 age range use AI tools at least weekly, and nearly half of all Millennials and Gen Z use them every single day. Anyone whose business serves younger Montanans or out-of-state visitors is already being affected by this shift whether they know it or not.

## What Is GAIO?

GAIO stands for Generative AI Optimization. It is the process of structuring your website so that AI tools can find your business, understand what you do, and recommend you to the people asking.

Traditional SEO helps you rank on Google. GAIO helps you get cited by ChatGPT. A business can sit at the top of Google search results and still be completely invisible to AI tools. They are related but they are not the same thing.

## What Actually Makes a Business Show Up

AI tools recommend businesses that are easy to understand. That comes down to a few things: clear and specific information about your services and location, structured data on your website that tells AI tools exactly what your business does, accurate listings across Google and other directories, and content that answers common customer questions directly.

Most Montana businesses have not done any of this yet. That gap is exactly why getting ahead of it now matters.

## The Window to Act Is Open but It Won't Stay That Way

AI search is growing faster than any technology shift since the move to mobile. The businesses that show up in AI results six months from now are building that advantage today. In Montana's outdoor, hospitality, and food and beverage industries where younger travelers make up a huge share of the customer base, being the business ChatGPT recommends instead of your competitor could be the difference between a full season and an empty one.
